Career Hightlights

Most recently, I have worked for Deloitte Consulting, LLP for about 2.5 years. There I served as both a Specialist Master, and Project Delivery Manager serving our Fortune 500 client base. Both are manager-level roles, however I was quite hands-on with a number of interesting projects ranging from AWS-based Big Data & Analytics platform build and deployment automation to leading the charge for a successful AWS MSP Competency partner audit. At Deloitte, I was engaged in the full client service delivery life-cycle, including design, build, operate, and optimize. While Deloitte had iniatives for Azure, GCP, and Oracle cloud, my projects were all AWS-based.

Much of my career thus far has been based in the context of delivering managed services for cloud-hosted workloads. This would include things like backup and disaster recovery planning, 24-hour monitoring and response, Event/Incident/Problem/Change Management, cost optimization, security reviews, IT transformation and cloud migration activities, etc. I have either led, or co-led 4 AWS MSP Competency audit preparation efforts (each spanning 3-6 months), so if you are thinking of a consulting partnership with AWS I can help!
